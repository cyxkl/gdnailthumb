How to add thumbnails to your YouTube videos

Mobile Tutorial

PC:

1. Whenever you upload a video, locate the panel where it says "Thumbnail" and click on Upload thumbnail.

2. Select the image that you want to use as the thumbnail photo, then press open.

3. Complete the other stuff you need for uploading, then press save (located on the bottom right corner).

Enjoy your thumbnail
